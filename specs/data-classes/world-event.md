# Data Class: world-event

## Description
A significant external event that affected Daniel, his community, or the broader world — recognizable by a specific date, real-world incident, or news event that had lasting relevance (Daniel engaged with it, it affected UT/his workplace, or it shaped context for future decisions).

## Actions
- save-vector

## Notes
- Must have clear relevance to Daniel or his context — generic world news that Daniel merely heard about is `news-digest`
- Distinguished from `news-digest` (raw unengaged news) and `ephemeral` (point-in-time data)
- Examples: Austin shooting affecting UT community, major incidents where Daniel's employer sent communications
- Include: date, what happened, who was involved, how it connected to Daniel
