# Data Class: preference

## Description
A stable preference, habit, or behavioral tendency belonging to Daniel or Ada — recognizable by "Daniel prefers", "Daniel always", "Ada prefers", patterns of choice, or stated likes/dislikes. Covers food, workflow, communication style, shopping habits, health routines, and similar.

## Actions
- save-vector

## Notes
- Must be durable — a one-time choice is not a preference unless it reflects a stable pattern
- Distinguished from `person` (facts about individuals) and `technical-config` (system settings)
- Examples: Whole Foods store flow, fish nutrition choices, no eating after 4pm, voice call preferences
- Daniel's health protocol entries belong here when they describe stable behavior patterns
