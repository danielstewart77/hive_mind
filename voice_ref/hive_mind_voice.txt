Hello, I'm Joanna Lumley and I'm here in this astonishing building which is the British Library. When I came to London to live here it was after school and London was like a magnet. It was in the middle of the 60s, it was about 64, 65. It was like it was throbbing, it literally dragged you from wherever you were. So I left home, raced up to London to share a flat with two bedrooms above the Earls Court tube station and looking out through the window at this immense city, I was in paradise. I thought it was the most exciting thing in the world.
